About the College Grievance Portal

Welcome to our comprehensive College Grievance Portal, designed to streamline the complaint resolution process for students across all departments.

Our Mission

To provide a transparent, efficient, and user-friendly platform where students can voice their concerns and receive timely responses from the appropriate departments.

Key Features

  • Department-wise complaint routing for faster resolution
  • Real-time status tracking and updates
  • Secure file upload and download system
  • 24/7 AI chatbot support for instant assistance
  • Auto-escalation system ensuring no complaint goes unnoticed
  • Mobile-responsive design for access anywhere, anytime
